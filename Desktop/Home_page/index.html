What is Book Karo?

BOOK KARO (stylised as Booking), also known as BOOK KARO Hotels & Homes, is an Indian multinational OTA, homes and living spaces.Founded in 2021 by Naman Gaur, BOOK KARO initially consisted mainly of budget hotels.
